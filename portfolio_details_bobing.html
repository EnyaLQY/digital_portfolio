VR Project for AR/VR Design Course

Mid-Autumn Festival is a traditional Chinese holiday for family reunions. However, there are hundreds of thousands of people who are far from their home due to their heavy workloads and the inconvenience of long-distance traveling. Whatâ€™s worse, due to the outbreak of covid in my home province, most of the in-person activities are prohibited even if people are living in the same community. As a result, a lot of people fail to celebrate the festival and play Bo Bing game together.

In order to make the game more intriguing and bring people closer than ever, our team is trying to develop a virtual reality solution to improve the playing experience of a Chinese folk dice game Bo Bing!

Working prototype is coming up!
