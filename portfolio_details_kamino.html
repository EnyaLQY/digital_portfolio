KaminoAuto Website


Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Company: Kamino Auto Industries Limited
  • Timeline: 2022.3 - 2022.5
  • Position: Website Designer
  • Tools: SiteGround, WordPress, Photoshop
  • Official Website: Kamino Auto

Summary

Kamino Auto Industries Limited is a professional B2B exporter for various auto spare parts and accessories in China.

The goal of this website is to manage and list products and connect with more clients internationally. According to the traffic analysis, the website brings an average monthly 2k+ unique visitors and 10k+ pageviews, and the activities is keeping increasing now.

Website Setup

The company provided the list of product categories and assets (company introduction, contact, photos, etc.). Based on these information, I selected several item listing templates that can best present the company and its product.

Given the limited budget and styling preference, I consulted with the company director to select the most suitable host plan and website theme.

UI/UX Improvements

To realize the product filtering and searching, I utilized Bootstrap plugins so that it could easily handle hundreds of products by their types, applications, and brands.

The product images provided by the company were in different sizes. It was challenging for the company to unify all the image sizes before uploading them to the database. To keep the listing consistent, I modified the CSS attribute to resize and cover each grid, which makes the future maintenance of the website easier.

Website Security and Updates

After the website domain was migrated successfully and site was encrypted by SSL certificate, the website is safe to be accessed all over the world.

The final deliverable includes the website host, WordPress account, and detailed documentation which helps the company workers who have little knowledge of WordPress edit and manage the product easily.
