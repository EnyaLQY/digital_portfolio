Digital Media Studies Capstone - WelList

With the galloping development of the internet and social media, many applications exist with similar functions and little difference. Based on our group's interviews with various people, we found that many people meet the problem of selecting suitable apps based on their needs.

Our team is aiming to develop a website to review apps that can help people track their health status, develop a healthy lifestyle, and promote mental well-being, listing apps' functionalities to help users narrow down their choices and choose the most suitable for them.

We have polished the preliminary proposal and currently we are working on the low-fidelity prototype and testing with potential users. More progress will be released soon.
